Health & Safety

Environmental hazards and infrastructure risks near a property. These checks flag issues, not score them.

  • EPA Superfund (SEMS)
  • EPA EJScreen
  • EPA Toxic Release Inventory
  • EPA Underground Storage Tanks
  • FHWA Traffic Counts (HPMS)
  • DHS Power Transmission Lines
  • FRA Rail Network
  • FEMA Flood Zones

Education

School district boundaries, performance data, and individual school locations.

  • Census TIGER School Districts
  • State Departments of Education (varies by state)
  • NCES School Locations (2022-23)

Area Context

Demographic and community data shown as reference information, never scored.

  • Census American Community Survey

NestCheck also uses live data from Google Maps, OpenStreetMap, and Walk Score for venue discovery, transit, and walkability scoring. These sources are available for all addresses and are not shown in the table above.
