HOW WE SCORE

NestCheck evaluates six daily-life factors, each scored 0–10. The scores are combined and normalized to a 100-point scale. Bonus points (up to +15) may be added for parking, outdoor space, or extra bedrooms.

85–100Exceptional Daily Fit
70–84Strong Daily Fit
55–69Moderate — Some Trade-offs
40–54Limited — Car Likely Needed
0–39Significant Gaps

Proximity checks for gas stations, highways, and TRI facilities are safety flags that don't affect your numerical score. Road noise, estimated from nearby road proximity, does contribute to your score — quieter locations score higher.
