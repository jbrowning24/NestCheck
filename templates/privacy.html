Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2025

NestCheck (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the NestCheck website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Property Addresses

When you submit an address for evaluation, we collect that address and generate a neighborhood evaluation report. We do not associate addresses with personal identities. Evaluation reports are stored as snapshots accessible via unique URLs.

Payment Information

When you purchase an evaluation, payment is processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your credit card number, expiration date, or CVC on our servers. Stripe handles all payment data in accordance with PCI DSS standards. We receive only a transaction confirmation and the last four digits of your card for reference. For details on how Stripe handles your data, see Stripe’s privacy policy.

Cookies

We set a single cookie, “nc_vid,” which is a randomly generated visitor identifier used to associate evaluation requests with your browser session. This cookie contains no personal information and is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of the service.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels.

Usage Data

Our server logs may temporarily record IP addresses and request metadata for operational purposes such as error diagnosis and abuse prevention. These logs are not associated with individual identities and are not retained long-term.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate neighborhood evaluation reports for the addresses you submit
  • Process payments for evaluation purchases
  • Diagnose technical issues and prevent abuse of the service

Third-Party Services

To generate evaluation reports, we send the address you submit to the following third-party services:

Google Maps Platform — for geocoding, nearby places, walking and driving distances, and transit information. Google’s privacy policy

OpenStreetMap (Overpass API) — for road classification and green space data. OpenStreetMap’s privacy policy

Walk Score API — for walkability, transit, and bike scores (when available). Walk Score’s privacy policy

These services receive only the property address and geographic coordinates necessary to generate the report. No personal information is sent to these services.

Data Retention

Evaluation snapshots are retained for up to 12 months from the date of generation. Snapshots may be deleted after this period without notice.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with the third-party services listed above, solely to generate your evaluation report, and with Stripe to process payments.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of a specific evaluation snapshot by contacting us at jab2493@columbia.edu. Include the snapshot URL in your request.

Children’s Privacy

NestCheck is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at jab2493@columbia.edu.
